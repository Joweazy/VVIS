Welcome to 'Vlak voordat ik sterf (translation: 'Moments Before I Die'), a unique Spotify playlist with over 5000 tracks. Crafted and nurtured since 2018 by a group of four Dutch friends, this playlist is a musical journey filled with the finest gems spanning various genres. Immerse yourself in an eclectic mix of obscure and alternative tracks carefully selected to create an unforgettable auditory experience. Join us on this sonic adventure as we celebrate the beauty of music that transcends the mainstream, making each moment resonate with meaning and emotion.
